OUR MEMBERS

THE BOYZ (더보이즈) is a 11-member South Korean boy group under ONE HUNDRED, consisting of Sangyeon, Jacob, Younghoon, Hyunjae, Juyeon, Kevin, New, Q, JuHaknyeon, Sunwoo, and Eric. They debuted on December 6, 2017, with their first mini album, THE FIRST.
